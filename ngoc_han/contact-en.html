Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out the contact form above, sending an email, or calling directly. I will respond within 24 hours to arrange a convenient time for you.

Where do the lessons take place?

I offer in-person lessons at my teaching space in Cam Le District, Da Nang City, as well as online lessons via Zoom or Skype, depending on your preferences and location.

How long is each lesson?

The standard lesson is 90 minutes, but I also offer 60-minute and 120-minute options depending on your needs and schedule.

What is your cancellation policy?

I require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Lessons cancelled within 24 hours will incur a 50% charge of the lesson fee.

Do you provide learning materials?

Yes, I provide all the necessary learning materials tailored to your needs and goals. These materials are included in the lesson fee.
