Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out the contact form above, sending an email, or calling directly. I'll respond within 24 hours to arrange a convenient time.

Where do the lessons take place?

I offer both in-person lessons at my teaching space in District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, and online lessons via Zoom or Skype, depending on your preference and location.

How long are the lessons?

Standard lessons are 90 minutes, but I also offer 60-minute and 120-minute options depending on your needs and schedule.

What is your cancellation policy?

I request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Lessons canceled with less than 24 hours' notice may be charged at 50% of the lesson fee.

Do you provide learning materials?

Yes, I provide all necessary learning materials tailored to your specific needs and goals. These are included in the lesson fee.
