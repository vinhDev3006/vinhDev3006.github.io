Teaching Services

Personalized English lessons tailored to all levels and goals

My Teaching Methods

Cognitive Method

Developing language skills through critical thinking and problem-solving activities, which stimulate the brain.

Communication Method

Focusing on practical communication through interactive activities and using language in a natural way.

Personalized Learning

Adjusting the teaching method according to individual learning styles, needs, and goals to achieve the best results.

My teaching methods combine proven traditional approaches with innovative techniques, creating an engaging and effective learning experience. I believe that a supportive learning environment, where students feel comfortable experimenting and making mistakes, is key to language development.

IELTS Preparation

My comprehensive IELTS preparation program is designed to help you achieve your desired score through targeted practice and personalized feedback. I focus on all four sections of the exam: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

The Program Includes:

  • Initial assessment to identify strengths and areas for improvement
  • Personalized study plan based on your goals and available time
  • Intensive practice with real exam materials
  • Detailed feedback and scoring based on official IELTS criteria
  • Effective test-taking strategies and time management skills

Course Duration:

4-12 weeks (depending on target score)

Learning Format:

Individual or small group (2-3 students)

Business English

My Business English program is designed for professionals who need to communicate effectively in an international business environment. Whether you're preparing for a presentation, negotiation, or daily communication at the workplace, this course will help you build confidence and master the language.

Key Topics Include:

  • Writing business emails and correspondence
  • Participating in and leading meetings
  • Presentation and public speaking skills
  • Negotiation skills and persuasive language use
  • Industry-specific vocabulary and terminology

Course Duration:

Flexible (10, 20, or 30 sessions)

Learning Format:

One-on-one or corporate group training

Conversational English

My Conversational English program focuses on improving fluency, confidence, and natural expression in everyday situations. This course is particularly suitable for those who want to enhance their speaking and listening skills for travel, social interactions, or daily use of English.

Program Highlights:

  • Interactive speaking activities and role-playing exercises
  • Pronunciation and natural intonation practice
  • Everyday vocabulary and common phrases
  • Cultural context and using language appropriately
  • Confidence-building techniques and practical communication practice

Course Duration:

Flexible (weekly or bi-weekly sessions)

Learning Format:

One-on-one or small group conversations

Additional Services

Children's Program

Engaging and fun English lessons for children aged 6-15, focusing on building a solid foundation through games, stories, and interactive activities.

  • Age-appropriate materials and activities
  • Focus on listening and speaking skills
  • Regular progress reports for parents

Age Group: 6-15 years old

Academic English

In-depth classes designed for students preparing for studying abroad or writing academic papers. Develop the necessary skills to succeed in an English-speaking academic environment.

  • Essay writing and research skills
  • Note-taking and lecture comprehension skills
  • Academic vocabulary development

For: High school students and university students

English for Travel

A short-term course designed for travelers who need practical English to communicate in international travel situations. Learn how to confidently handle common travel scenarios.

  • Vocabulary related to airports and transportation
  • Communication at hotels and restaurants
  • Common phrases and emergency situations

Duration: 2-4 weeks

Conversation Club

Weekly group sessions aimed at developing conversation skills in a comfortable and supportive environment. Practice with fellow group members under the guidance of a professional instructor.

  • Weekly discussion and debate topics
  • Practice pronunciation and fluency in speaking
  • Cultural exchange and social learning

Group size: 4-6 participants
