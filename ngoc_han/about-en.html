My Journey

My passion for teaching English began during my university years, when I volunteered as a tutor for children and students of all ages. Witnessing their progress and growing confidence inspired me to pursue a career in education.

While studying at the University of Foreign Languages, I worked at several language centers in Da Nang City, gaining valuable experience teaching diverse groups of learners. In 2020, I became an IELTS examiner, which gave me deeper insight into assessment criteria and effective preparation strategies.

In 2025, I founded my own English class to offer personalized teaching methods tailored to each learner’s style and goals. Since then, I’ve helped hundreds of students achieve meaningful milestones—whether in academic improvement, career development, or expanding their ability to communicate internationally.

Teaching Philosophy

"I believe language learning should be engaging, practical, and inspiring. My goal is not just to teach English—but to empower students to express themselves confidently in a new language."

My teaching approach is built on four core principles:

Personalization

Every student has unique goals, needs, and learning styles. I adapt my methods to suit each individual.

Real-World Application

Language is a tool for communication. I focus on practical usage rather than abstract theory.

Cultural Context

Understanding culture is key to mastering a language. I incorporate cultural elements to enhance learning.

Confidence Building

A supportive learning environment encourages students to take risks and grow. I create a safe space to practice and improve.

Professional Development

I’m committed to lifelong learning and continuously updating my teaching practices. I regularly attend workshops and training programs to stay current and bring the best to my students.
