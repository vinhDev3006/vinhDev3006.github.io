My Journey

My passion for teaching English began during my university years when I volunteered as a tutor for underprivileged children. Witnessing their progress and growing confidence inspired me to pursue teaching as a career.

After completing my Master's degree in Linguistics, I worked at several language centers in Ho Chi Minh City, gaining experience with diverse student populations and teaching methodologies. In 2016, I became an IELTS examiner, which deepened my understanding of assessment criteria and effective preparation strategies.

In 2018, I established my own teaching practice to provide more personalized instruction tailored to individual learning styles and goals. Since then, I've helped hundreds of students achieve their language learning objectives, whether for academic advancement, career development, or personal growth.

Teaching Philosophy

"I believe that language learning should be engaging, relevant, and empowering. My goal is not just to teach English, but to help students discover their voice in a new language."

My teaching approach is built on these core principles:

Personalization

Every student has unique needs, goals, and learning styles. I tailor my teaching methods to match individual requirements.

Practical Application

Language is a tool for communication. I focus on real-world usage rather than abstract theory.

Cultural Context

Understanding cultural nuances is essential for true language mastery. I integrate cultural insights into all lessons.

Confidence Building

Creating a supportive environment where students feel comfortable taking risks is crucial for language acquisition.

Professional Development

I am committed to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest research and methodologies in language teaching. I regularly attend conferences, workshops, and training sessions to enhance my skills and bring fresh perspectives to my teaching practice.
