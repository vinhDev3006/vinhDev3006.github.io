My Teaching Methodology

Cognitive Approach

Developing language skills through problem-solving and critical thinking activities that engage the mind.

Communicative Method

Focusing on real-world communication through interactive activities and authentic language use.

Personalized Learning

Tailoring instruction to individual learning styles, needs, and goals for maximum effectiveness.

My teaching methodology combines proven approaches with innovative techniques to create an engaging and effective learning experience. I believe in creating a supportive environment where students feel comfortable taking risks and making mistakes, which is essential for language acquisition.

IELTS Preparation

My comprehensive IELTS preparation program is designed to help you achieve your target score through targeted practice and personalized feedback. I focus on all four sections of the exam: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

What's Included:

  • Diagnostic assessment to identify strengths and areas for improvement
  • Customized study plan based on your target score and timeline
  • Intensive practice with authentic exam materials
  • Detailed feedback and scoring using official IELTS criteria
  • Effective strategies and time management techniques

Duration:

4-12 weeks (depending on target score)

Format:

Individual or small group (2-3 students)

  • Presentation skills and public speaking
  • Negotiation and persuasive language
  • Industry-specific vocabulary and terminology

Duration:

Conversational English

My Conversational English program focuses on building fluency, confidence, and natural expression in everyday situations. This course is perfect for those who want to improve their speaking and listening skills for travel, social interactions, or general communication.

Program Highlights:

  • Interactive speaking activities and role plays
  • Natural pronunciation and intonation practice
  • Everyday vocabulary and idiomatic expressions
  • Cultural context and appropriate language use
  • Confidence-building techniques and practice

Duration:

Flexible scheduling (weekly or bi-weekly sessions)

Format:

Individual or small conversation groups
